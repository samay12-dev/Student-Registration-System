STUDENT REGISTRATION SYSTEM - INSTRUCTIONS:

1. FILL OUT THE FORM
   - Enter your full name in the "Name" field
   - Enter a secure password in the "Password" field
   - Enter your valid email address in the "Email" field
   - Enter your Student ID in the "Student Id" field

2. REGISTER
   - Click the "Register" button to add your information to the records
   - All fields must be completed before registration
   - If a field is empty, an alert will notify you

3. VIEW RECORDS
   - Your registered information will appear in the table
   - The table displays Name, Email, and Student ID
   - Previously registered students are listed in the records section

4. NOTES
   - Ensure your email is in valid format
   - Student ID should be your official university ID
   - Data is stored in the table for your session
